Dark rum base
=============

151 Reasons
-----------

*   3/4 oz 151 proof rum
*   2 oz orange juice
*   2 oz 7-Up® soda
*   3/4 oz lemonade

A Furlong Too Late
------------------

*   2 oz light rum
*   4 oz ginger beer
*   1 twist lemon peel

-----------------------

*   1 oz light rum
*   1 oz anejo rum
*   1 oz orange juice
*   1/2 oz lemon juice
*   3 oz ginger ale
*   1 twist lemon peel

Adam
----

*   2 oz dark rum

Amy's Tattoo

Aruba Rum Punch

*   2 dashes bitters

Aunt Agatha

Bermuda Rum Swizzle
